Advertising and Marketing Policy

  1. By adding your items to your encomi account and therefore to your website, you grant encomi a license to use it. This does not mean that we claim any ownership of your content, we are just allowed to display your content and use it in the following ways to promote encomi and your business.

  2. General advertising on social media

    1. To enhance the reach of all encomi-shops we will get in contact with social media users that acquired a certain follower number. They will receive a discount code and/or link to give to their followers.
    2. The amount of the discount as well as the frequency and other details are not yet determined. We will get in contact with you to have your agreement and clarify details before taking any steps forward.
    3. For those buyers who do not have the discount code, all products will have the regular price.

  3. Shop-specific advertising (not yet implemented)

    1. encomi offers you the possibility to promote your items on our page.
    2. On the one hand, this will include the display of your shop on our home page where there are some selected shops suggested to the buyer. As this space is limited, we will use an algorithm that keeps changing between the sellers. For you, this means that we cannot guarantee that your shop will be displayed on the home website at all times even though you applied for this promotion.
    3. One the other hand, it includes a promotion on our shop website where the products from all shops are displayed. If you applied, some of your items will be pushed, so that they are on top of the page for the buyer. This does not just apply to the “All” page but to all categories. If there are several sellers registered for this promotion, we will again use an algorithm that changes between all, so we cannot guarantee that your items will be displayed on top of the page.
    4. For information about pricing see our Fee&Payment Policy.
    5. We reserve the right to refuse a promotion request if there is a given reason.

 

Behavior and values

1.     Age

  1. You have to be 18 years or older to be a seller on our

2.     Privacy

  1. Protect your privacy. This includes avoiding to share private information on your profile or in contact with your buyers like e.g. personal contact details.
  2. Buyers have the possibility to contact you via E-Mail when they use the “message seller” button on your item page. You can specify the E-Mail used for this on your dashboard.

3.     Honesty and Transparency

  1. We as encomi appreciate transparency in sellers. This means an accurate presentation and description of your items, including information about the origin and production of items, materials etc.
  2. Be upright about who you are. To avoid fake identities, we ask you for your full name when you are registering as a seller, this information will not be given to anyone else but us. Find more information about how we use your information in our Privacy Policy.

4.     Reliability

  1. Your buyers rely on you. This means they expect reliant information about the products you make, like e.g. which materials are used, where they come from, how they are made; they rely on the quality you exhibit on your shop. The same goes for shipping, so please make sure to meet the indicated shipping time, when receiving returns refund the money in time and so on. Have in mind that happy customers are customers that are likely to come back or recommend your shop to others.
  2. We reserve the right to terminate your use of encomi if there are increased complaints about you. However, we will contact you to try to resolve the issue before taking any action.

5.     Anti-discrimination

  1. encomi prohibits any kind of discrimination against someone based on their personal characteristics. These include color, ethnicity, origin, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disabilities etc. Discrimination includes behavior/treatment as well as language concerning any kind of interaction with other users on encomi like e.g. the refusal of service or the expression of intolerance or disrespect because of their personal attributes.
  2. If we believe your communication, shop content or else contains any kind of discrimination against certain group(s), we reserve the right to deactivate your account temporarily or permanently in case of repeated discrimination.
  3. If you notice discriminative behavior from someone else, please contact us via email so that we can take care of it as soon as possible.

6.     Communication

  1. We want to create a safe space for everyone, this does not only include anti-discrimination but also a respectful communication in general. When in contact with other users, stay friendly and humble. This means not using insults, disrespectful language etc.

General information on the registration, your account and your content

1.     Becoming part of encomi

There are two ways to become a part of encomi:

  1. We contact you personally with the invitation to join our platform. As a next step, you fill out the form on our website “Become a seller” which includes your personal data like e.g. your email address and your username, but most importantly the preferences for your website. This helps us to build your website the way you want it, customized to your unique brand, so that you have the best selling experience while having as little effort as possible. As a next step we will send you the finished template of your website, so that you can check whether everything is to your satisfaction. As soon as we have your approval, your website will go live and your shop will be added to encomi.co. For the upcoming steps see Your content.
  2. You apply for encomi via the “Become a seller” form. The general procedure is the same as for invited sellers but before we start designing your website, we will check/examine if your products and your shop match the requirements for our encomi sellers. That is why we ask you to either send us your Instagram profile, your already existing shop on another platform or website, or anything else (e.g. pictures) from where you sold your products up to this point. At the moment encomi is only for selling jewelry and similar, but you can come back to us at a later point in time as we plan to expand our product range in a later stage.

2.     Costs

    1. You can use encomi without paying any recurring fees. Therefore, the only costs for you are the transaction fee of 3.9%. To read more about that go to our Fees & Payment Policy.
    2. If you register for your own domain additional costs will occur, see Your domain.
    3. Also, the promotion comes with additional, monthly costs, you can also read more about this in our Advertising and Marketing Policy as well as the Fees and Costs Policy.

3.     Your domain

  1. As our seller, you have the option to either get a domain from us for free which contains your username/a chosen name from you and encomi (yourname.encomi.co) or you can acquire an independent domain. Your domain name has to comply with our terms of use, e.g. it cannot contain any kind of hatred. Also, we cannot guarantee that the domain chosen by you will be available, in that case we will contact you and offer alternatives.
  2. If you would like to acquire your own domain please contact us via Email.
  3. The billing starts on the day we activate the domain for you.
  4. If you would like to change your choice later on, you can contact us via Email. This will be effective at the end of your billing period.
  5. An already made payment for that month cannot be refunded. The same goes if you delete your account, we cannot refund you the costs for your domain for that month.
  6. In some cases, we offer you your down domain for free for a certain amount of time. We will inform you beforehand when the billing would start and that you have to cancel at least one day beforehand if you do not want to continue using our services.

4.     Your personal shop policies

  1. In general, our policies also apply to your shop, however, you have the possibility to have your own shop policies including shipping information, return policies etc. that give the buyer further information how your shop works.
  2. These can be added and edited on your dashboard under settings.
  3. When first registering you have to provide us with your return conditions before we can activate your website and account.

 

5.     Your content

  1. Your responsibility

    1. You are solely responsible for your content; you confirm that you have the rights for your content and you are not harming any third parties’ rights or disrespecting our terms of use.

  2. Your website and your shop on encomi

    1. Your encomi-shop and your website are inevitably connected, this means that every item and category you have on encomi will also be displayed on your website. You can manage everything on your encomi-dashboard, this will automatically apply to your website as well.

  3. Adding your listings

    4. When the registration process is finished and you have gotten access to your account by us, you can start uploading your items via the dashboard. There are two ways to add items to your shop:

    1. You use our “Import data” button which will automatically add all the items you have uploaded on Etsy to your shop. Afterwards you can still change descriptions, categories or other details on the dashboard.
    2. The second option is uploading the items yourself, also via the dashboard. This includes pictures of the item, a title, the price, a potential discount (by using the fields for old price and new price), a description with all needed details, potential style options etc. You can still do this after importing the data in the first option.
    3. In any case, you are obligated to use your own photos and phrasing, and provide accurate information about the item.

  4. Putting items on sale

    1. As your Etsy shop is connected to your encomi shop, the items you have on sale on Etsy will also be on Sale on encomi as soon as you use the sync button on your dashboard.
    2. You can also put items on sale manually by creating an old price and a new price in your listings.

  5. Deleting items

    1. To ensure a proper functioning of our and your website you cannot delete items that have been bought at least once. Nevertheless, you have the possibility to either deactivate them (which removes them from your shop but is not messing with our database, and you still have them on your dashboard) or mark them as “out of stock” which does not remove them from your shop but prevents buyers to put them in their cart.

 

6.     Checkout and payment

    1. Your items can be purchased through the encomi website as well as your own website. In both cases, you will get a notification with the items bought as well as shipping information. You can also see all information regarding orders on your dashboard.
    2. You are obligated to ship the products within a reasonable amount of time (the time given in your personal shipping policy) and then mark them as shipped on your dashboard so your buyer knows when to expect their order.

7.     Deactivation and deletion of your account

    1. We do not have a period of notice for encomi, so if you want to, you can delete your account at any time. Be aware that deleting your account also deletes your website. Within 30 days after deleting your accounting we store your data on our server, which gives you the possibility to reactivate your account within that time frame.
    2. The same goes for a temporary deactivation due to a violation of our terms of use, if your account has been deactivated for more than 30 days, it will be permanently deleted and you can no longer use our services even by creating a new account.

  1. encomi is not a secondhand-platform but one for selling handmade products. This means that selling products that are not crafted, made, designed (or similar) by you, your team or your employees are not allowed. We reserve the right to delete items or deactivate your account in case you are not complying with this guideline.
  2. Furthermore, there are certain items we do not want to have sold on encomi. If we have reason to believe the items sold in your shops fall in any of those categories, we reserve the right to temporarily or permanently deactivate your shop and website.
  3. These items are prohibited on our websites:
    • Alcohol, tobacco or any kind of drugs
    • Certain animal products (not including leather or fabric made from animal material) or human remains
    • Dangerous items or items that promote violence like e.g. weapons
    • Items supporting hatred, discrimination etc.
    • Illegal items, items promoting legal activity and highly regulated items
    • Nudity and other pornographic content
  4. There can be exceptions for each category, so if you are not sure whether the items you would like to sell on our platform fall into these categories, please contact us via our message form.
  5. If you suspect another user to sell items in one of those categories, please also use the possibility to contact us, so that we can investigate and possibly deactivate the account of that seller.

All of those have different shipping options, shipping costs, trade restrictions when shipping across borders and so on. As a seller you are responsible to choose the shipping option that is of most benefit for you as well as your customer while complying with laws, trade regulations etc.
